Rocketry

I devoted many hundreds of hours to McGill Rocket Team as Aerostructures Lead, Technical Director, and Avionics developer. Those hours will forever remain some of my best (... and stressful) memories. In 2018, after 3 years of disappointing competition results, our team became overall champions of the Spaceport America Cup out of 124 international universities - a true team effort.
